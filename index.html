The Business Value of OPA

Automated Statistical Engine

OPA entirely removes the need to export data to SAS, JMP, or R. It executes massive statistical pipelines automatically natively, autonomously selecting between One-Way ANOVA, Welch's t-test, or Kruskal-Wallis based on mathematical dataset structures.

Instant Economic Forensics

Calculates the exact Margin Over Feeding Cost (MOFC) instantly. Isolate the true financial ROI of a dietary supplement over the control group using our highly accurate total cost model incorporating weighted phase-feeding economics and pathogenic opportunity cost.

Advanced Feed Formulation

Evaluate custom feed formulas against published nutritional specifications of specific genetics companies (Aviagen, Cobb-Vantress, etc.). Validates critical amino acid ratios relative to Standardized Ileal Digestible (SID) Lysine.

Zero Platform Lock-In

Crucially, all data remains structurally owned by the facility and can be bulk-exported back to Excel (CSV) at any time. Designed to operate securely on a local environment or a private cloud architecture.
